Heart and Home

Changing the world for the better

Hi!

We are the Heart and Home team, we strive to protect stray animals who dont have a home. Life is very easy for a normal dog or cat. They never have to worry about being hungry, They live a very comfortable life, their owners they even give them a bath! These comforts are immidietly lost when an animals loses its home, This is very common especially in India

How can you help?


You might think that to help you would have to some kind of superhero or you would require a huge invesment. Infact the opposite is true. It is not possible to bring every stray to your house and keep it as a pet. But it takes very little effort to buy some pet food and put it in a bowl on the streets. I am telling you the dogs will love it! You can even contact organizations like PETA
